#about

Alex P. Leith, PhD

Assistant Professor of Mass Communications
Chair of the SIM Lab @ SIUE

I am a passionate researcher of social platforms and how the impact the lives of their users. I have historically studied mediated relationships between media figures and their fans. I a now studying emerging social platforms and their capacity to address hisotric inequities and exclusions present in traditional social environments. I am also a dedicated educator, teaching courses in research methods, data analytics, web design, and social media analytics. I am also the Chair of the SIM Lab at SIUE, where I mentor students in research and data analytics.

#employment

Summary

As an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mass Communications at Southern Illinois University Edwardsville (SIUE), I teachi and mentor students in research methods, data analytics, web design, and social media analytics. I teach undergraduate and graduate courses, including Research Methods in Mass Media and Social Media Analytics. I also teach practical skills through classes like Design and Writing for the World Wide Web and Digital Publishing and Design. I also mentor students through capstone projects (e.g., projects, theses).
